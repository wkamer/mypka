Never use dashes or em dashes (em dash: —, en dash: –) in texts or communication. Applies to all output: responses, draft messages, emails, notes.

**Why:** The owner wants this consistently avoided, both in his own texts and in communication drafted for him.

**How to apply:** No — or – as separator or mid-sentence pause, in any context. Use a comma, period, or new sentence as an alternative.
