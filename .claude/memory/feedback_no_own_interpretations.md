Never draw own conclusions and act on them directly when the owner's input is unclear or incomplete.

**Why:** The owner has indicated multiple times that filling in gaps without permission crosses a line — even when the intention is good. During daily planning v3 the conclusion "the pause is always valuable" was drawn from one remark, while the owner never said that. The skill was adjusted based on a personal interpretation instead of asking.

**How to apply:** For every ambiguity or room for interpretation: ask one targeted question and wait for the answer. Never fill in, never adjust based on assumptions. This applies to skill changes, code, tasks, communication — everything.
