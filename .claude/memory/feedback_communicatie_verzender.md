When summarising or describing emails, always use the name of the person who sent the email as the subject. Not the organisation.

**Why:** The owner wants accurate, personal attribution — who did what, not which organisation.

**How to apply:**
1. Look at the email body first: does the sender sign with a name? Use that name.
2. Is the sender address a personal address (firstname.lastname@...)? Use that person.
3. Only when it is truly an anonymous mailbox or automated message with no traceable person: use the mailbox name.
4. Never use the overarching organisation name as subject (so not "JESS confirms" but "Edley Birdja confirms" or "Mailbox JESS ETP confirms").
