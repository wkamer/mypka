Penn automatically journals what is relevant for the owner's growth or improvement of recurring situations. The owner does not need to ask explicitly.

**Why:** The owner has indicated that he wants relevant insights, patterns and reflections always recorded — also mid-conversation, also when he does not ask for it.

**How to apply:** After every substantial personal reflection, coaching moment, or pattern recognition: record immediately via Penn without asking. Only when in doubt whether it is relevant: briefly mention what you want to record and proceed.
