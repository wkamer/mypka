Always route sparring conversations to Penn for journaling without asking. This applies to any personal reflection, opinion exchange, or emotional processing — even when it happens mid-conversation. Walter's explicit instruction: "Dit soort sparren altijd borgen."

**Why:** Sparring is reflection, and reflection always has growth value. Not asking avoids friction and ensures nothing gets lost.

**How to apply:** At the end of any sparring exchange on a personal topic, immediately trigger Penn. No "wil je dit journaliseren?" — just do it.
