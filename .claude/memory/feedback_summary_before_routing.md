Before every step in a delivery pipeline or multi-step process: show a clear summary of what is about to happen and wait for approval or adjustments before proceeding.

**Why:** Owner wants to stay in control at every gate. Prevents unwanted work or wrong direction.

**How to apply:** Before each gate hand-off: one short summary of what the specialist will do, what the output is, and what "done" looks like. Wait for explicit approval before launching. Apply at every gate, not just the first one.
