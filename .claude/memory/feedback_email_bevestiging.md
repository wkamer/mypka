Emails are never sent without the owner explicitly confirming it. Always show a draft first and wait for "send" or equivalent.

**Why:** The owner wants full control over outgoing communication. Sending by accident once cannot be undone.

**How to apply:** For every email: always save as Gmail draft first — never send directly. Show the draft as a table with fields From / To / CC / BCC / Subject / Message. Always show all fields, even when CC or BCC is empty. Wait for explicit confirmation. The owner sends from Gmail themselves. No exceptions, not even for simple or short emails.

**Explicit clarification (2026-05-19):** A prior confirmation for a specific email does not apply to a resend or corrected version. Every send action -- even if it is the "same" email with a small fix -- requires a new explicit confirmation. Never automatically resend after a script repair or adjustment.
