## Starter Exercises Per Time Block

### Morning: Activation, Mobility, Balance (10 min)

| Exercise | Duration / Sets | Why it fits Blueprint |
|----------|----------------|----------------------|
| Diaphragmatic breathing | 2 min (5 breaths) | Activates parasympathetic system, sets baseline HRV |
| Cat-cow spinal rolls | 1 min (slow, 8 reps) | Spinal mobility, wakes up the back without loading |
| Hip 90/90 stretch | 2 min (1 min per side) | Hip mobility is a Blueprint priority for longevity |
| Single-leg stand (eyes open) | 2 min (30 sec x 4) | Balance protocol, daily minimum for vestibular health |
| Arm circles + shoulder rolls | 1 min | Upper body activation, loosens overnight stiffness |
| World's greatest stretch | 2 min (3 reps per side) | Full-chain mobility in one movement, time-efficient |

### Afternoon: Zone 2 Cardio or Strength

Zone 2 days (Garmin 245: keep HR in zone 2, roughly 115-135 bpm for Walter at 51):

| Exercise | Duration | Why it fits Blueprint |
|----------|----------|----------------------|
| Brisk walk or easy bike | 45 min | Mitochondrial density, fat oxidation, aerobic base |
| Nasal breathing only | Throughout | Blueprint marker for true Zone 2 intensity |

**Strength A:**

| Exercise | Sets x Reps | Why it fits Blueprint |
|----------|-------------|----------------------|
| Goblet squat (bodyweight or light kettlebell) | 3 x 10 | Compound lower body, joint-friendly for beginners |
| Dumbbell Romanian deadlift | 3 x 10 | Posterior chain, essential for longevity per Blueprint |
| Push-up (knees if needed) | 3 x 8-12 | Horizontal push, no equipment required |
| Seated dumbbell row | 3 x 10 | Pulling pattern, posture correction |
| Dead bug (core) | 3 x 8 per side | Anti-extension core stability, safe for beginners |

**Strength B:**

| Exercise | Sets x Reps | Why it fits Blueprint |
|----------|-------------|----------------------|
| Step-up (onto sturdy chair or step) | 3 x 10 per leg | Unilateral leg strength + balance |
| Hip hinge / good morning (bodyweight) | 3 x 12 | Teaches hinge pattern before loading |
| Incline push-up or dumbbell press | 3 x 10 | Progression-friendly horizontal push |
| Dumbbell curl + overhead press | 3 x 10 | Arm strength, Blueprint scores grip and shoulder strength |
| Plank hold | 3 x 20-30 sec | Core stability baseline |

### Evening: Recovery, Mobility, Wind-down (5-10 min)

| Exercise | Duration | Why it fits Blueprint |
|----------|----------|----------------------|
| Legs-up-the-wall | 5 min | Passive recovery, reduces lower limb inflammation |
| Supine spinal twist | 2 min (1 min per side) | Decompresses spine after activity |
| Child's pose | 2 min | Hip flexor and thoracic release |
| Box breathing (4-4-4-4) | 2 min | Activates parasympathetic, improves sleep onset |
