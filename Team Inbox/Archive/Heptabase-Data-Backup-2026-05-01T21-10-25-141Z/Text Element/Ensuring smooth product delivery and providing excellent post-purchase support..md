*Ensuring smooth product delivery and providing excellent post-purchase support.*

- **Phase 1: Supplier Management & Order Processing**

   - **Objective:** To automate and streamline the order flow from customer to supplier.

   - **Key Actions:**

      - Integrate dropshipping apps (e.g., Dsers) for automated order forwarding.

      - Establish clear communication channels with your supplier(s).

      - Regularly monitor supplier performance (stock levels, shipping speed).

- **Phase 2: Shipping & Tracking Management**

   - **Objective:** To provide transparency and confidence in the shipping process.

   - **Key Actions:**

      - Obtain and distribute tracking numbers promptly.

      - Implement automated shipping update notifications to customers.

      - Clearly communicate estimated delivery times on your store and order confirmations.

- **Phase 3: Proactive Customer Support & Returns**

   - **Objective:** To ensure customer satisfaction and manage any post-purchase issues effectively.

   - **Key Actions:**

      - Set up responsive customer service channels (email, chat, phone).

      - Prepare FAQs to address common inquiries.

      - Define and communicate a clear, customer-friendly return and refund policy.

      - Address order issues (delays, damages, non-delivery) swiftly and professionally.
