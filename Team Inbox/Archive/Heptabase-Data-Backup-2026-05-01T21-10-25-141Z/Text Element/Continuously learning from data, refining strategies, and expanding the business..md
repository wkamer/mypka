*Continuously learning from data, refining strategies, and expanding the business.*

- **Phase 1: Performance Data Analysis**

   - **Objective:** To measure and understand the effectiveness of all previous stages.

   - **Key Actions:**

      - Track key performance indicators (KPIs): conversion rate, AOV, CAC, ROAS, net profit, website traffic, customer lifetime value (CLTV).

      - Analyze ad campaign data (which ads, audiences, and channels are profitable).

      - Monitor website analytics (bounce rate, time on page, checkout abandonment).

- **Phase 2: Strategic Optimization**

   - **Objective:** To make data-driven improvements across the entire business funnel.

   - **Key Actions:**

      - Refine ad targeting, creatives, and budget allocation based on ROAS.

      - Optimize product pages and store flow for higher conversion rates.

      - Adjust pricing or offer bundles to maximize profit.

      - Improve customer service processes.

      - Continuously monitor competitor activities and adapt.

- **Phase 3: Scaling & Expansion**

   - **Objective:** To grow the business by increasing reach, revenue, and profitability.

   - **Key Actions:**

      - Increase advertising spend on proven winning campaigns.

      - Explore new advertising channels.

      - Expand your product line with related or complementary "winning products."

      - Consider offering premium shipping options.

      - Explore international market expansion.

      - Invest in advanced fulfillment solutions (e.g., private agents for better costs/speeds) as volume grows.
