# Health

**What this is:** a dimension of my life, not a goal. Health is something I tend to, not something I finish. This note exists to keep that distinction clear when I'm setting Goals or running Habits.

**How I think about it:** health here is broader than the medical version. It includes physical, mental, creative, and relational well-being. If any one of those is starving, the others start to leak.

**Signals I pay attention to:**
- energy at the start of the [[morning-build-session]] - if I'm dragging on day one of the week, something upstream is off
- whether I'm building things I care about, which is why [[ship-mvp-by-q3]] sits inside this Key Element
- recovery quality, not just sleep hours
- how often I say yes to things that don't feed any of the four dimensions

**Linked Habits:**
- [[morning-build-session]] - creative and mental health show up here first

**What this Key Element is not:** a tracker, a streak, or a number to optimize. It's the lens I use when I'm deciding whether a Goal or Project belongs in my life right now.
